1.4. Additional Resources

Foglight.org is the community site for Foglight users. In addition to more general topics on Foglight, a section of the site is reserved for custom agent development and includes blog entries, code examples, and a forum for interacting with other agent developers.

For those who require additional assistance, Dell offers consulting services to help customers with custom agent development. Service offerings are customized to the needs of the project and may include:

  • Project Scoping and Planning
  • Agent Design
  • Agent Implementation
  • Long-term Agent Support

Please contact your Dell sales representative for more information.

Developer's Kit defects and enhancements may be submitted to Dell Support using the support website, SupportLink (support.quest.com).
